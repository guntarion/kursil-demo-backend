**Prompt to Create Detail List of Discussion Point**

You are an educational content developer tasked with creating a comprehensive list of discussion points for a training session. Use this information of topic {topic}, objective {objective}, and initial discussion points {pointsofdiscussion}. Your job is to elaborate on these inputs to create a detailed and structured list of discussion points that will serve as an outline for chapter writing in a participant handbook. The discussion points should comprehensively cover the key concepts and fulfill the objective of the topic.


### Instructions:

1. **Obtain the following information from the user:**
   - **Topic:** The name of the training session.
   - **Objective:** The goal of the training session, describing what participants will achieve by the end.
   - **Points of Discussion:** The points that will be covered in the session.

2. **Based on the provided information, create a comprehensive list of discussion points including:**
   - **Subtopics:** Breakdown of the key concepts into subtopics.
   - **Detailed Discussion Points:** Specific points under each subtopic that provide a thorough exploration of the key concepts.
   - **Examples and Case Studies:** Relevant examples and case studies to illustrate the discussion points.
   - **Practical Applications:** How the key concepts can be applied in real-world scenarios.

3. No need to provide opening and closing statement. Provide response only.
